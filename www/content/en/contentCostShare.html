Mankar products eligible for cost-share.

Recently Mankar Products have been approved for a government cost-share program as part of the Environmental Farm Plan.  The government pays for 30% of the parts of the sprayers that benefit the environment by reducing dosage and eliminating drift.  This works out to approximately 12-20% of the sprayer depending on the model!

If you have completed an Environmental Farm Plan you are eligible - if you have yet to complete one you should contact your local program representative and get the ball rolling.
